Crispy Tofu Bowl

A household favorite! This is a simple tofu recipe that takes few ingrediants and is perfect for a busy week night. Modify any of the toppings for a unique bowl everytime.

Ingrediants

  • 1 16-ounce block extra firm tofu
  • 4 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Press and drain the tofu
  2. Cut the tofu into 3/4 inch cubes. In a large bowl, combine soy sauce, nutritional yeast, sriracha, and olive oil. Season with black pepper. Add tofu cubes, toss, and marinate at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat the oven to 400 degrees F. Place tofu cubes in an even layer on oiled baking sheet. Bake 30-40 minutes until golden, flipping half way.
  4. While tofu is baking, make white rice.
  5. Serve tofu over rice, garnish with sesame seeds and chopped scallions.
